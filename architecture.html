Bond featurizer#

[KME19]

\[\begin{split}e_{m} &= \frac{ m^{2}(m+2)^{2} }{ 4(m+1)^{2} + 1 } \\ d_{0} &= 1 \\ d_{m} &= 1 - \frac{ e_{m} }{ d_{m-1} } \quad (m \geq 1) \\ f_{m}(r) &= (-)^{m} \frac{ \sqrt{2}\pi }{ r_{c}^{3/2} } \frac{ (m+1)(m+2) }{ \sqrt{ (m+1)^{2} + (m+2)^{2} } } \left( \mathrm{sinc} \left( (m+1)\pi \frac{r}{r_{c}} \right) + \mathrm{sinc} \left( (m+2)\pi \frac{r}{r_{c}} \right) \right) \\ h_{0}(r) &= f_{0}(r) \\ h_{m}(r) &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{d_{m}}} \left( f_{m}(r) + \sqrt{\frac{ e_{m} }{ d_{m-1} }} h_{m-1}(r) \right) \quad (m \geq 1) \\\end{split}\]

\(m = n_{\max} l_{\max}\)

Three-body to bond#

\[\begin{split} \tilde{\mathbf{e}}_{ij} &= \mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{sigmoid}}(\mathbf{v}_{k}) \odot \left( \sum_{ k \in \mathcal{N}_{i} \backslash \{ j \} } j_{l}(z_{ln} \frac{r_{ik}}{r_{c}} ) Y_{l}^{0}(\theta_{jik}) f_{c}(r_{ij}) f_{c}(r_{ik}) \right)_{l=0, \dots, l_{\max}-1, n=0, \dots, n_{\max}-1} \\ \mathbf{e}_{ij} &\leftarrow \mathbf{e}_{ij} + \mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{swish}}(\tilde{\mathbf{e}}_{ij}) \odot \mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{sigmoid}}(\tilde{\mathbf{e}}_{ij}) \\\end{split}\]

\(j_{l}\) is the \(l\)th spherical Bessel function with roots at \(z_{ln} \, (n=0, \dots, n_{\max}-1)\). \(Y_{l}^{0}\) is the spherical harmonics with \(m=0\). \(\mathcal{L}_{\sigma}\) is a one-layer perceptron with activation function \(\sigma\).

Spherical Bessel function#

The spherical Bessel function of the first kind

\[\begin{split} % DLMF 10.49.3 j_{0}(z) &= \frac{\sin z}{z} \\ j_{1}(z) &= \frac{\sin z}{z^{2}} - \frac{\cos z}{z} \\ j_{l+1}(z) &= \frac{2l+1}{z} j_{l}(z) - j_{l-1}(z) \quad (l \geq 1) \quad (\mbox{DLMF 10.51.1}) \\ j_{n}(z) &\approx \frac{z^{n}}{(2n+1)!!} \quad (z \to 0) \\\end{split}\]

The derivative of spherical Bessel function of the first kind

\[\begin{split} j_{0}'(z) &= -j_{1}(z) \\ j_{l}'(z) &= j_{l-1}(z) - \frac{l+1}{z} j_{l}(z) \quad (l \geq 1) \quad (\mbox{DLMF 10.51.2}) \\\end{split}\]

Spherical harmonics with \(m=0\)#

\[\begin{split} Y_{l}^{0}(\theta) &= \sqrt{ \frac{2l+1}{4\pi} } P_{l}(\cos \theta) \\ \frac{d }{d \theta} Y_{l}^{0}(\theta) &= \sqrt{ \frac{2l+1}{4\pi} } \frac{d}{d \theta} P_{l}(\cos \theta) \\\end{split}\]

This definition adopts Condon-Shortley phase.

Legendre polynomial (DLMF 14.10.3)

\[\begin{split} P_{0}(x) &= 1 \\ P_{1}(x) &= x \\ (n + 1) P_{n+1}(x) - (2n+1) x P_{n}(x) + n P_{n-1}(x) &= 0 \quad (n \geq 1)\end{split}\]

Derivative of Legendre polynomial

\[\begin{split} \frac{d}{d x} P_{n}(x) &= n P_{n-1}(x) + x \frac{d}{dx} P_{n-1}(x) \quad (n \geq 1) \\ \frac{d}{d \theta} P_{n}^{(m)}(\cos \theta) &= -\frac{1}{\sin \theta} \left( (n+m) P_{n-1}^{(m)}(\cos \theta) - n \cos \theta P_{n}^{(m)}(\cos \theta) \right) \\\end{split}\]
